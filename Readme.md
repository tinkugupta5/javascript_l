9.  What three main things does `createSlice` automatically generate?
10. How do you define initial state within `createSlice`?
11. How do you define synchronous reducers within `createSlice`? Provide a small example.
12. What are "immer" and "immerjs" and how do they relate to Redux Toolkit?

14. How do you access the action creators generated by `createSlice`?

20. What is a "slice" in Redux Toolkit terminology?
